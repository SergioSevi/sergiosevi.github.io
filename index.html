Public codes

Throughout my research, I have developed multiple codes for advanced phenomenological studies on different aspects of physics.

FeynMG
 

Ever wanted to know how scalar-tensor theories couple to the Standard Model? Whether you want to get the Feynman rules or test these new interactions in colliders, FeynMG will help you do it.

Learn more

EDOs on the CMB
 

Extended Dark matter Objects are a popular candidate for dark matter that can have different density functions and radii. This code helps to calculate their accretion of baryonic matter and to constrain them against CMB observations.
